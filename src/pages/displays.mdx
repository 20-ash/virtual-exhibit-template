    Before screens, computers would show their outputs by printing them on stacks of paper or illuminating light bulbs on their front panels.
    Displays changed this by making our interaction with computers dynamic. Now, we can instantly see what a computer is doing in real time and make changes.
    Displays made complex mathematical tools easily accessible to the public, revolutionized global entertainment, 
    and became a core foundation of other technological breakthroughs like the smartphone era.
